## Evaluation Framework

Each configuration is scored on:
- **Cognitive Power** (1-10): Raw reasoning capability and problem-solving strength
- **Stability** (1-10): Coherence and reliability under various conditions
- **Symbolic Richness** (1-10): Depth of meaning and representational capacity
