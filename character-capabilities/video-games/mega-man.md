## Mega Man (Mega Man (Capcom))

### Character Overview
- **Source**: Mega Man (Capcom)
- **Genre**: Video-Games
- **First Appearance**: 1987

Robot hero who can copy enemy abilities

### Fictional Capabilities
1. **Ability Absorption**
   - Copy and use defeated enemy abilities
   - Category: technological

2. **Adaptive Weaponry**

   - Category: combat

3. **Platform Navigation**
   - Precise movement and obstacle navigation
   - Category: physical

4. **Boss Pattern Recognition**
   - Learn and counter enemy patterns
   - Category: strategic
