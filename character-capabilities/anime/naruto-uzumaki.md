### Fictional Capabilities

1. **Shadow Clone Jutsu (Kage Bunshin no Jutsu)**
   - Create multiple physical copies that share experiences
   - Each clone can act independently
   - Knowledge and experience transfer back to original upon dispersal
   - Enables parallel learning and multi-tasking

2. **Rasengan**
   - Concentrated sphere of rotating chakra
   - High-power focused energy attack
   - Requires precise control and focus
   - Can be enhanced with elemental variations

3. **Sage Mode**
   - Enhanced perception and power from natural energy
   - Increased awareness of surroundings
   - Amplified physical and mental abilities
   - Connection to environmental state
